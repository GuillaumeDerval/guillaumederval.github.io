Supervision

During my postdoctoral research, I had the opportunity to co-supervise several PhD students.
  • Shadan Ghafari (starting soon) - Parametric optimization on MILPs for energy systems modeling.
  • Baptiste Istace (ongoing) - Counterfactuals for energy systems modeling.
  • Ahmed Tayachi (ongoing) - LLMs for transparency of large energy models.
  • Antoine Larbanois (ongoing) - Lead-cooled fast reactor SMR integration into the Belgian Energy Landscape.
  • Bardhyl Miftari (done 2025) - Sensitivity analysis for linear changes of the constraint matrix of a (mixed-integer) linear program.

  • Samy Aittahar (ongoing) - Optimal Control of Renewable Energy Communities subject to Network Peak Fees with Model Predictive Control and Reinforcement Learning Algorithms.
