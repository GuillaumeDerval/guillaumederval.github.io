Supervision

  • Ahmed Tayachi (co-supervisor, ongoing) - LLMs for transparency of large energy models.
  • Antoine Larbanois (informal, ongoing) - Lead-cooled fast reactor SMR integration into the Belgian Energy Landscape.
  • Samy Mokeddem (informal, ongoing) - Distributed e-Fuel Hubs (DEFH).
  • Bardhyl Miftari (informal, done 2025) - Sensitivity analysis for linear changes of the constraint matrix of a (mixed-integer) linear program.
  • Amina Benzerga (informal, done 2025) - Hosting capacity evaluation.
  • Jocelyn Mbenoun (informal, ongoing) - Modeling of the 2050 Belgian Energy System
  • Samy Aittahar (informal, ongoing) - Optimal Control of Renewable Energy Communities subject to Network Peak Fees with Model Predictive Control and Reinforcement Learning Algorithms.
